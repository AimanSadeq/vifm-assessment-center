# Technical Competency Framework - Accounts Payable (worked example)

> Status: **DRAFT for approval.** Accounts Payable is the worked example; the same
> structure is the template for any finance function (FP&A, GL, Treasury, Tax, …)
> and for client-custom functions derived from a job description.

## 1. Purpose & use cases
A professional, online-assessable competency framework for **technical (functional)**
ability - not behaviour. Two uses from the same instrument:
- **Hire / screen** candidates for an AP role (objective, role-relevant).
- **Evaluate / develop** an existing team (find gaps, target training).

## 2. The model (per the agreed correction)
```
Domain (Finance)         grouping only - NOT assessed, NOT banded
  └─ Function (Accounts Payable)   a JOB / role - NOT assessed, NOT banded
        └─ Competency               THE ASSESSED UNIT - banded Basic / Intermediate / Advanced
              └─ Skill               what a question measures; skills roll up to the competency
```
- A delegate sits the **Accounts Payable** assessment (the function/job).
- Questions measure **skills**; skills roll up to a **competency score (%)**.
- Each **competency** is banded **Basic / Intermediate / Advanced** (thresholds: < 60 / 60-84 / ≥ 85).
- There is **no** overall "Accounts Payable: Advanced" or "Finance: Advanced" band.

## 3. Accounts Payable - the competencies (assessed units)
Six competencies cover the general AP role. Each lists the **skills** it is measured by
and what **Basic / Intermediate / Advanced** looks like (used in the report).

### C1. Invoice Processing & Matching
*Capturing, coding, and matching supplier invoices and resolving exceptions.*
- **Skills:** invoice capture & coding · 2-way vs 3-way match · PO / GRN matching · discrepancy & exception resolution · duplicate-invoice detection
- **Basic:** processes clean PO invoices; knows 2- vs 3-way match.
- **Intermediate:** resolves common mismatches (price/qty/GRN) independently.
- **Advanced:** handles complex exceptions, partial/consolidated invoices, and sets matching tolerances/policy.

### C2. Supplier / Vendor Management
*Vendor master integrity, statement reconciliation, and query handling.*
- **Skills:** vendor master data integrity · supplier statement reconciliation · query & dispute resolution · onboarding & validation · bank-detail change controls
- **Basic:** updates vendor records; reconciles simple statements.
- **Intermediate:** resolves disputes and reconciling items; validates new vendors.
- **Advanced:** owns master-data governance and detects vendor-fraud red flags.

### C3. Payments & Disbursement Controls
*Executing payment runs under proper approval and segregation of duties.*
- **Skills:** payment-run execution · payment methods (WPS / SWIFT / cheque) · approval workflow & limits · segregation of duties · payment-fraud controls
- **Basic:** prepares a payment run; follows the approval steps.
- **Intermediate:** manages run timing, methods, and exceptions; applies SoD.
- **Advanced:** designs controls, prevents duplicate/fraudulent payments, optimises cash timing.

### C4. AP Accounting & Period Close
*Accruals, coding, sub-ledger-to-GL reconciliation, and month-end.*
- **Skills:** accruals & cut-off · GL / cost-centre coding · AP sub-ledger to GL reconciliation · aged-payables analysis · month-end close tasks
- **Basic:** codes invoices to GL; books simple accruals.
- **Intermediate:** reconciles the sub-ledger and prepares month-end AP.
- **Advanced:** owns AP close, cut-off accuracy, and aged-payables / DPO analysis.

### C5. Tax & Regulatory Compliance
*Indirect tax, withholding, e-invoicing, and policy compliance on payables.*
- **Skills:** input VAT treatment · withholding tax · e-invoicing (GCC: ZATCA / FTA) · T&E & expense-policy compliance · documentation & audit trail
- **Basic:** applies VAT codes; keeps required documentation.
- **Intermediate:** handles WHT and e-invoicing requirements correctly.
- **Advanced:** ensures end-to-end compliance and advises on edge cases.

### C6. Controls, Risk & AP Systems
*Internal controls, audit readiness, and ERP / automation proficiency.*
- **Skills:** P2P internal controls · audit readiness · fraud schemes & prevention · ERP AP-module proficiency · AP automation / OCR & reporting (aging, DPO, SLAs)
- **Basic:** follows controls; uses the ERP AP module for core tasks.
- **Intermediate:** supports audits and uses automation/reporting day to day.
- **Advanced:** strengthens controls, leads audit response, and improves the AP process.

## 4. Scoring & banding
- Each question maps to one **skill**; each skill maps to one **competency**.
- Competency score = correct / total across its skills' questions → **%**.
- Band per competency: **Basic < 60 · Intermediate 60-84 · Advanced ≥ 85**.
- The report shows each competency's band + score + the matching descriptor above,
  plus development pointers. (No function/domain-level band.)

## 5. Customisation from a job description
Default content = general AP (above). When a client supplies a JD, the JD extractor
proposes which competencies are in scope and may add/rename competencies and skills to
the client's role - producing a **custom technical assessment** mapped to the same model.
